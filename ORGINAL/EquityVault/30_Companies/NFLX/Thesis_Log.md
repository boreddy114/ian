### InterPositive Patent Risk

Public framing: post-production tooling (wire removal, reframing, relighting). Patent language: "a computing system for enhancing existing video LLMs with advanced filmmaking capabilities" enabling users to "generate video content for films/movies without the need for actual filming." Claims 20% reduction in schedule/physical production and 50% VFX cost reduction. The patent grants broader capability than publicly claimed — the constraint is policy, not physics. Watch deployment scope through credits, behind-the-scenes coverage, and union filings.

## 2026-02-21 — Initiation: Digital Scarcity in an Abundance World

**Source:** ACTIVE_NFLX_Winning Aggregator in AI Era.docx; IC_Memo.md
**Rating:** Under Review

### Core Thesis

**Digital scarcity in an abundance world.** The WBD IP catalog (DC, Harry Potter, GoT, HBO library) functions as the equivalent of a 50-year land bank — a durable, irreplaceable asset that fits our framework for the AI era even without a physical tether. Content IP is digitally scarce when the catalog is deep and exclusive enough.

**Non-extractive = long runway.** Netflix's low cost per viewing hour (~$0.14 vs. competitors at $0.40-0.50) reflects a business that is *not extractive* — it creates far more value than it captures. Per the Marathon framework, companies that leave surplus on the table typically have long growth runways. The key isn't churn sensitivity per se; it's that Netflix has room to extract more over time without breaking the value proposition.

### Bull Case: Valuation & Expectations

- **DD revenue growth:** Ad tier scaling ($3B → $8.5B by 2030e), international expansion, pricing power
- **Huge operating leverage:** Content spend falls from 42% → 32% of revenue by 2030e while absolute spend grows — ~1,000bps EBITDA margin expansion
- **FCF conversion upside:** 20% FCF margin (2025e) → 33% (2030e); if WBD deal falls through, Netflix returns to net cash glide path and capital returns narrative

At ~29x forward earnings vs. 5yr avg of ~35x, expectations are compressed. The standalone business is a 20% EPS compounder priced for modest outcomes.
