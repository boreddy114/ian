## 2026-04-28 — F2Q26 Earnings: Beat and Raise, VAS Validates the Thesis

**Source:** DRAFT V Q2FY26 Beat and Raise.docx; Q2-2026-Earnings-Release_vF.pdf; Visa-Inc-Second-Quarter-2026-Financial-Results-Presentation.pdf; BMO initiation (April 21, 2026, OP/$365); Evercore preview (April 28, 2026, In-Line/$340)
**Rating:** Buy

### Thesis Established

Visa is a scale-economies-shared business with a four-party network moat that AI is making wider, not narrower. The thesis: VAS scales above 20% as Visa monetizes data, brand, and trust assets across an installed base it can't lose. Consensus underwrites Visa as a 9-10% revenue compounder; the right number is 11-13%. The F2Q26 print is the first hard data that forces the gap to close.

### VAS at 30% of Revenue, 27% CC Growth

VAS revenue reached $3.3B in the quarter. AI-embedded products are leading adoption:
- **Visa LLM:** Proprietary Large Transaction Model built on billions of internal transactions. Delivering "up to 5x increase in fraud value capture."
- **Fastest-adopted products:** Smarter Stand-In Processing, Visa Provisioning Intelligence (VPI), Visa Consumer Authentication Service (VCAS), Visa Advanced Authorization, Visa Risk Manager.
- First earnings call where AI is a clear revenue driver inside Visa, not a defensive talking point.

CMS revenue grew 24% CC, well above Investor Day combined VAS+CMS frame of 16-18%. Some one-time items per Suh (deal timing, performance adjustments) but underlying trend materially above prior bar.

### Pismo: Wells Fargo Signs

Wells Fargo committed to migrate to Pismo core banking ledger — first major US bank on the platform. Qualitatively different from prior fintech-issuer wins. Added France, Philippines, Paraguay, Romania (15 new countries since acquisition). Pismo revenue books in Other and counts as VAS, contributing to the +41% Other revenue line.

### Stablecoin & Agentic Commerce

- **Stablecoin:** 160 programs globally, payments volume +200% YoY, $7B annualized settlement run-rate across 9 blockchains. Five new chains added (Arc, Base, Canton, Polygon, Tempo). Visa is a validator on Tempo and super-validator on Canton.
- **Agentic:** Launched Intelligent Commerce Connect and Visa CLI (command-line interface for AI agent payments). McInerney: trust is the limiting factor, not compute. Bridge layer "has very similar economics to the products we have today" — incremental volume without margin compression.

### Capital Allocation

$7.9B Q2 buyback (largest in company history) + $1.3B dividends. New $20B authorization on top of $13B remaining = ~$33B total capacity. Buying at ~19.5x 2-year forward non-GAAP EPS — bottom of L4Y -1SD per BMO. Management buying aggressively at the low end of the historical valuation band.

### Risks to Monitor

1. **Cross-border structural decel.** If May-June doesn't recover to 11-12% as Ramadan adjustment implies, bear narrative reasserts. The single most important thing to watch into Q3.
2. **Q3 EPS decel optics.** MSD-HSD growth is mechanical but market may not parse it.
3. **VAS cyclicality.** Some Q2 strength is one-time (Olympics, deal timing). FY27 normalization to 18-20% reduces upside.
4. **Payments nationalism.** Wero, digital euro, European sovereignty. Caps multiple expansion — manage via position sizing, not avoidance.

### Upgrade/Downgrade Conditions

- **Upgrade conviction:** May-June cross-border at 11-12% CC; Q3 EPS at high end of MSD-HSD with Q4 acceleration confirmed; at least one more Tier-1 Pismo customer in 12 months.
- **Downgrade:** Cross-border ex-intra-EU below 10% CC for two consecutive months in Q3; VAS below 20% CC in any quarter without offsetting acceleration; explicit guide cut on Q3 print.
