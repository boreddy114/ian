## One-Liner

ADP is misclassified as a software company. It is regulated financial infrastructure that uses software as its interface. The moat is layered — the payroll engine (Layer 1) is replicable; the financial infrastructure (Layer 2), fiduciary/risk transfer (Layer 3), and distribution (Layer 4) are not. AI disruption attacks Layer 1, which is the least important layer.

## Layered Moat Framework

| Layer | Description | Moat Strength | AI Vulnerability |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 — Payroll Engine | Rules-based tax/withholding calculations | **None** — codifiable, replicable | High |
| 2 — Financial Infrastructure | $3.1T annual money movement, 78M W-2s, tax filing, money transmission licenses | **Strong** | Low |
| 3 — Fiduciary & Risk Transfer | Largest SMB 401(k) admin (190K plans), PEO co-employer (158K WSEs), fiduciary duty | **Strongest** | Very Low |
| 4 — Distribution & Trust | 67K employees, 1.1M clients, 75+ year brand, 80% of Fortune 500 | **Real but eroding** | Moderate |

**Catalini Moat Screen:** FORTRESS classification. Classical score 4.2/5, Agentic vulnerability 2.0/5. ~55–60% of switching costs fall in Category B (coordination/compliance = durable, non-automatable) vs. enterprise SaaS median of ~30–40%.

## Bull Case

1. **Moat is deeper than the market prices.** Market sees "payroll software" and applies AI disruption discount. The financial infrastructure + fiduciary layers are not software problems and are not addressable by AI.
2. **Valuation compressed to historic lows.** ~17.8x NTM P/E — lowest in 5+ years, below COVID trough. A 10–11% EPS grower with record retention priced like a utility facing secular decline.
3. **Q3 FY26 beat-and-raise.** Revenue +7%, margin expansion accelerating, PPC improved to +1%, retention at records, pricing contribution 130bps (up from 100bps).
4. **AI efficiency measurable.** RUN platform 8% reduction in client contacts. India year-end: 35% call volume reduction + 35% labor reduction. The Zone deploying to 40%+ of service population.
5. **Lyric SI partnerships.** Now working with Accenture, EY, KPMG — directly addressing the enterprise go-to-market gap vs. Workday.
6. **Buyback accelerating.** Management buying stock aggressively at ~18x NTM.

## Bear Case / Honest Weaknesses

1. **AI employment displacement is directionally real.** Not visible in data today (Anthropic Economic Index shows AI concentrated in coding ~1–3% of US employment), but 2–3 year timeline could compress.
2. **Seat-based model exposure.** Mid-market/upmarket ES is heavily seat-based. PPC sensitivity: 1pt change ≈ ~20bps ES revenue impact.
3. **CEO profile risk.** Maria Black is a 30-year ADP veteran from sales. Right for stable execution; may not be right if AI disruption accelerates into business operations in 18–24 months.
4. **Data moat unquantified.** Management could not articulate why 27M records produce better AI than 7M records. If competitors ship indistinguishable AI features, the data advantage argument collapses.
5. **FY27 bookings unknown.** Won't get FY27 guide until fall. If bookings come in at low end (4%) and PPC reverts to flat, constructing 6% ES revenue growth becomes very difficult.
6. **Dual-variable risk.** Recession or AI disruption could hit both client count and employee count simultaneously.

## Why Not Upgrading

1. AI disruption risk is directionally real even if not yet visible
2. FY27 bookings guide is the critical unknown (won't have until fall)
3. Macro could deteriorate to hit both demand variables simultaneously
4. CEO not positioned for a tech-forward pivot if needed

## Key Monitoring

1. **PEO vertical mix data** — verify management's claim PEO is NOT concentrated in white-collar professional services (weakens Jefferies bear thesis if confirmed)
2. **Q4 FY26 bookings** — biggest quarter. Any narrowing upward from 4–7% range is catalyst
3. **AI feature parity** — if Dayforce/Paycom ship indistinguishable AI features despite 3–4x fewer records, data moat collapses
4. **Rippling PEO buildout** — the real competitive threat (matching co-employment + superior tech)
5. **Anthropic Economic Index** — track whether AI agent usage expands beyond coding into business ops/HR/finance
6. **CEO succession/innovation capacity** — monitoring point if environment shifts

## Read-Across Value

Even as a non-holding, ADP is the best real-time labor market signal in the portfolio:
- ES PPC = broad employment health
- PEO pace-per-control = white-collar SMB health
- Client fund balances = aggregate payroll dollars flowing through economy
- Pricing trends = HCM pricing power (read-across to PAYX, PCTY, PAYC)
