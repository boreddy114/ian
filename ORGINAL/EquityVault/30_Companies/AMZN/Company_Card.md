## One-Liner

AMZN is mispriced as a capex-heavy cloud catch-up name when it is a vertically integrated AI infrastructure business with the open-platform cloud distribution moat, accelerating AWS on a $150B run rate, and all-time-high consolidated op margins — without an AI premium baked in.

| Advertising | $17.2B | +22% Y/Y | High incremental margin |
| Other (Leo, Devices, etc.) | Early | — | Pre-revenue / investment |

## Bull Case

1. **AWS reacceleration** — 28% Y/Y, fastest in 15 quarters. Backlog $364B ex-Anthropic (~$464B+ inclusive). Capacity-constrained, not demand-constrained.
2. **Open-platform Bedrock moat** — Best-in-class agent-to-agent orchestration. OpenAI + Anthropic both committed to AWS. Bedrock customer spend +170% Q/Q.
3. **Operating leverage** — All-time-high 13.1% op margin. AWS margin 37.7%. Retail cost-to-serve leveraging (units +15% vs. fulfillment +9%).
4. **Optionality stack** — Leo commercial launch Q3 (Delta, JetBlue, AT&T, NASA). Whole Foods +100 stores. Prime price hike mid-2026 (~$5B annualized). Trainium racks-as-product.

## Bear Case / Honest Weaknesses

1. **Trainium silicon is weaker than call language implied** — Experts say T2 benchmarks against Nvidia H100, not H200/B200. Anthropic routes <1% of inference to Trainium. Software ecosystem "where AMD ROCm was 3 years ago."
2. **FCF collapsed** — TTM FCF $1.2B (–95% Y/Y). Management frames as AWS-launch playbook but compression is severe.
3. **"Top 3 chip business" includes Graviton + Nitro** — Trainium-specific revenue is materially smaller than the $20B run rate headline.
4. **Racks-as-product structurally constrained** — Trainium physically locked into Nitro/Neuron toolchain; not a merchant silicon business.

## Valuation

- ~25x '26E EBIT, ~20x '27E EBIT (Arete)
- Arete SOTP $301 → ~18% upside from $255
- ~57% of EV is AWS at 9.0x '26E sales
- No AI premium vs. MSFT/GOOG peers
- No capital return program (anomaly vs. mega-cap peers)

## Catalyst Path (12 months)

1. Anthropic + OpenAI capacity online 2H26 → AWS reaccel into FY27 (Arete models 34%)
2. Bedrock Managed Agents GA + first material agentic revenue
3. Prime price hike (mid-2026, ~$5B annualized)
4. Leo commercial launch (Q3)
5. FCF stabilizing as capex/sales peaks in FY26
6. Any buyback or dividend initiation

## Sell Triggers

- AWS growth deceleration in Q2 or Q3
- OpenAI or Anthropic diversifying compute away from AWS at scale
- FCF remaining ≤$1B TTM through Q3:26
- Retail margin compression exceeding AWS/ads EBIT contribution

## Key Monitoring

- AWS growth trajectory (quarterly)
- Trainium adoption — watch Anthropic inference routing
- FCF inflection timing
- Bedrock Managed Agents traction
