## One-Liner
MELI is a verification-grade fintech-marketplace ecosystem whose durable moat is the credit data flywheel and Mexico POS coordination position — not the commodity marketplace network the consensus is pricing.

## Bull Case
1. **Credit data flywheel is verification-grade (Tier 5):** $12.5B portfolio, NPL falling 300bps over 3 years to 4.4% while book grew 90% YoY — model accuracy improving at scale.
2. **Mexico POS coordination position emerging:** Installed base "approaching all incumbents combined" — potential Visa-of-LatAm-payments.
3. **Marketplace bifurcation favors MELI:** Verified ecosystem buyers (credit holders, MELI+, Pago MAU) growing while only commodity discovery cedes to Temu/Shein.
4. **NPS leadership in 4 countries (commerce + fintech):** Historic milestone in 2025 — functional brand compounding.
5. **Capital allocation aligned with moat-deepening:** 5-6 ppts margin invested deliberately; management explicitly uses "moat" language.
6. **CAP gap ~2-2.5x:** Market-implied 3.5 years vs. framework-supported 7-9 years. Consensus PT $2,600-2,850 (~+45-58% upside).

## Bear Case / Honest Weaknesses
1. **Temu surpassed MELI in Mexican e-commerce traffic (Q2'25 Similarweb):** Commodity discovery layer under active attack from Asian platforms.
2. **10-K explicitly acknowledges low barriers to entry and multi-homing risk** — company's own words, stated twice.
3. **FORTRESS classification is borderline:** Classical 3.56 barely above 3.5 threshold; Agentic Vulnerability 2.40 barely below 2.5 threshold.
4. **Credit concentration risk:** Anticipation-of-receivables business in Brazil flagged as "massive risk" by AlphaSense expert community.
5. **CEO transition untested under stress:** Szarfsztejn took over Jan 1, 2026 — first 12 months are a watch item.
6. **Argentina margin normalization:** 37% direct margin will revert as hyperinflation comps lap — optical, not structural, but affects reported numbers.
