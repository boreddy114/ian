### The Coordination Equilibrium (Why BR Is Special)
BR's keystone moat is NOT the SaaS lock-in — it's the regulatory franchise underlying street-name proxy services. Five stacked reasons the Fork Test fails conclusively:
1. **POA chain cannot be forked** — Each Nominee individually transferred proxy authorities via signed power-of-attorney
2. **DTC omnibus proxy cannot be forked** — Single omnibus proxy in the chain; bifurcating adds complexity without value
3. **NYSE fee schedule cannot be forked** — Rules establish specific economic structure for intermediaries
4. **Issuer relationships cannot be forked** — Issuers want one point of contact
5. **Social contract cannot be forked** — Common knowledge that BR is the focal point (Bitcoin analogy)

### Switching Cost Bifurcation (GTO)
- **Category A (automatable):** ~43% — schema mapping, workflow translation, integration rewiring, user retraining
- **Category B (durable):** ~57% — regulatory audit trail continuity, multi-counterparty coordination, compliance certification, ERISA fiduciary obligations
- Comparable to ADP (~55-60% Cat B); well above enterprise SaaS median (30-40%)
