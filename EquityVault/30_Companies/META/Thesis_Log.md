## April 29, 2026 — Q1'26 Earnings: +33% Revenue, Capex Hike Dominates Narrative

**Source:** META_Q1_2026_Call_Notes.docx

### Headline
Revenue $56.3B (+33% / +29% CC) — strongest growth in 5 years. Stock -7% intra-day on Q2 guide and capex raise. The market is pricing the spending, not the revenue.

### Revenue & Ad System
- **Value optimization suite ARR >$20B**, more than doubled YoY
- New **trillion-parameter adaptive ranking model** (LLM-scale) in production for ads inference
- +6% conversion lift on landing page view ads from LLaMA architecture advances
- +1.6% conversion lift from adaptive ranking expansion to off-site
- Susan Li: "a lot of work being done before we even incorporate more of the LLM work into our underlying ads ranking models" — more upside ahead

### Engagement
- **+10% lift in real time spent on Facebook** from Q1 ranking improvements
- +9% Facebook video watch time in US/Canada; total video +8% globally (largest Q/Q gain in 4 years)
- Doubled length of user interaction sequences used for Instagram model training in Q1
- Same-day posts now >30% of recommended Reels (>2x YoY)
- 500M+ users each on FB and IG watching AI-translated videos weekly

### AI Infrastructure — The Big Numbers
- **FY26 capex:** $125-145B (raised from $115-135B); midpoint $135B
- **$107B incremental contractual commitments in Q1** — mostly third-party cloud for 2026/2027 (first explicit acknowledgment of meaningful third-party cloud use)
- **1GW+ custom silicon** with Broadcom + "significant amount of AMD chips" alongside Nvidia
- End of Q1 estimated non-cancelable commitments ~$238B
- FY27: explicitly deferred but "continued to underestimate compute needs"

### Meta AI & Agents
- **Muse Spark / MSL:** First Meta Superintelligence Labs model released April; powers updated Meta AI. Double-digit % increases in sessions per user
- **Business AI:** 10M+ weekly conversations (10x since start of year). Currently free. Monetization deferred
- **AI Glasses:** Daily users tripled YoY. "Fastest growing categories of consumer electronics ever"
- Partnerships ads run rate >$10B, doubled YoY

### Headcount
- May RIF confirmed. Q1 ended at 77,986 (-1% Q/Q already)
- Susan Li: "we don't really know what the optimal size will be"

### Analyst Push-Back
- **ROI signposts on AI investment (Morgan Stanley):** Zuckerberg refused quantitative milestones
- **FY27 capex direction (Bernstein):** Susan deferred but conceded underestimation
- **Agentic monetization (Goldman):** Both personal agents and business AI monetization deferred
- **Recursive self-improvement (Barclays):** Zuckerberg — "coding is one ingredient, not the only thing." Distancing from OpenAI/Anthropic positioning
- **Open-source agent competition (Barclays follow-up):** Zuckerberg's lane is distribution at billions scale, not lab-first for thousands

### Key Unresolved Questions
1. When does business AI monetization begin? (Currently free, 10M+ weekly conversations)
2. What does FY27 capex look like? (Susan conceded they've underestimated needs)
3. Can ad revenue growth sustain 20%+ while capex grows 40%+?
4. Is custom silicon competitive with Nvidia on inference cost? (1GW deployed is significant)
5. Will personal agents transact (commissions/premium) in 2026 or is this a 2027+ story?
