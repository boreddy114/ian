## One-Liner

GOOG is no longer a single-engine Search business. Q1:26 demonstrated three engines — Search defending with resilient high-intent queries, Cloud accelerating to 63% growth with 33% op margin on TPU economics, and YouTube absorbing dollars flowing out of traditional Search. The vertical-integration moat (custom silicon + proprietary models + distribution) is the structural advantage.

## Bull Case

1. **TPU is genuinely competitive with Nvidia** — 1.4x better performance/dollar, 30% cheaper inference, 20–30% better energy efficiency. ~1M TPUs deployed for Anthropic training. Apple uses TPUs for foundational training. Meta committing tens of billions.
2. **Cloud margin expansion is structural** — 33% op margin driven by vertical integration and TPU cost advantages, not one-time mix. Experts describe as "structurally sustainable."
3. **Cloud winning incremental share** — Market share from ~8% to ~14–15%. Capturing ~40% of net new AI workloads in US/Canada. Backlog $462B (nearly doubled Q/Q). AI Solutions revenue +800% Y/Y.
4. **Search defending better than feared** — +19% accelerating. AI Overviews monetizing previously un-monetizable long/complex queries (coverage upside from ~20%). TAM expanding, not shrinking.
5. **Vertical-integration moat hardest to replicate** — Owns silicon, model, platform, distribution. Decade+ to replicate.

## Bear Case / Honest Weaknesses

1. **Search ad revenue trajectory more pressured than headlines** — Click growth essentially flat; revenue driven by CPC inflation (4–13% Y/Y). Some agencies report 10–15% Y/Y declines in clients' Google Search spend. Up to 60% of searches projected zero-click from AI Overviews.
2. **External TPU sales is NOT merchant silicon** — Runs through neoclouds (Fluidstack, TeraWulf, Cypher) as a strategic GCP capture feature. Small revenue in 2026; majority in 2027.
3. **Microsoft ecosystem lock-in is a real ceiling** — Enterprises in Windows Server / AD / M365 default to Azure. Copilot wins on M365 integration. GOOG wins workload types but not broad displacement.
4. **Regulatory overhang** — Antitrust remedies in Search + ad tech unresolved. Structural remedies on YouTube/ad tech remain tail risk into 2027.

- Adverse antitrust ruling forcing structural separation of YouTube, ad tech, or Search
- Anthropic shifting workloads materially away from TPU
