## 2026-05-01 — IC Memo: Buy Recommendation — High Conviction (Vertical Integration Thesis)

**Source:** IC Memo "Two Models, Two Buys: AMZN as Open Platform, GOOG as Vertical Integration" (May 1, 2026)
**Rating:** Buy — top-conviction long
**Price at write:** Post Q1 print, stock +6% on the day

### Core Thesis

GOOG is the **vertical-integration play** on secular AI infrastructure spend. Custom silicon (TPU v7), proprietary models (Gemini), tightly integrated platform (Vertex AI), distribution into Workspace and Search. Three distinct engines: Search defending, Cloud accelerating, YouTube absorbing. The vertical-integration moat — custom silicon + proprietary models + distribution — supports Cloud margin expansion and is what makes the full-stack pitch resonate with data-heavy enterprises.

### Why Conviction Is Higher Than AMZN

Three specific findings from expert pressure-test work:

1. **TPU is competitive; Trainium isn't (yet).** Multiple independent experts: TPUs are a generation behind Nvidia but winning decisively on cost (1.4x better perf/dollar, 30% cheaper inference). Anthropic has ~1M TPUs in production vs. <1% of inference on Trainium.
2. **Cloud margin expansion is structurally validated today.** GOOG's 33% Cloud margin is delivered and explained by structural drivers. AMZN's margin story is partly future-tense with execution risk on Trainium.
3. **Vertical-integration moat is harder to replicate.** GOOG owns silicon + model + platform + distribution = decade+ to replicate. AMZN's open-platform moat (governance, distribution, partners) is valuable but each component faces direct competition.

### What Expert Work Confirmed (Bull)

- TPU v7 (Ironwood): "one generation behind Nvidia" in raw compute but 1.4x better performance per dollar, 30% cheaper inference, 20–30% better energy efficiency. For deployments >1,000 chips, economic case becomes "profound."
- Anthropic ~1M TPU deployment validates frontier-scale viability. Apple uses TPUs for foundational training. Meta reportedly committing tens of billions.
- Cloud margin 33% (from 17.8% Y/Y) is "structurally sustainable" per experts — vertical integration + TPU cost differentials + AI Solutions mix shift.
- GCP market share moved from ~8% to ~14–15%. Capturing ~40% of net new AI workloads in US/Canada.
- JAX outperforms PyTorch on native TPU. "Torch TPU" lets PyTorch models run on TPUs with near-zero code changes via XLA. CUDA-as-only-option narrative weakening.

### What Expert Work Flagged (Bear)

- **Search ad revenue more pressured than Pichai's framing.** Click growth essentially flat; revenue driven by CPC inflation (4–13% Y/Y). Some agencies report 10–15% Y/Y declines in clients' Google Search spend. Up to 60% of searches projected zero-click.
- **External TPU sales is strategically limited.** Not merchant silicon — runs through neoclouds (Fluidstack, TeraWulf, Cypher). GOOG generally NOT selling physical chips for enterprise on-prem. Adjusted expectations accordingly.
- **Microsoft ecosystem lock-in is a real ceiling.** Enterprises in Windows Server / AD / M365 default to Azure. Copilot wins on M365 integration.
- **Regulatory overhang into 2027.** Previous rulings less severe than feared, but structural remedies on YouTube/ad tech remain tail risk. Caps multiple expansion.

### Key IC Caveat

GOOG carries asymmetric tail risk on Search disruption that AMZN does not carry on retail. If AI-driven query erosion accelerates faster than Cloud + YouTube absorb the dollars, consolidated growth compresses. We are underwriting that absorption pace holds — highest-impact assumption in the GOOG case.

### Position Sizing

Top-conviction long. Funded by reductions in less-defensible AI infrastructure beneficiaries. Time horizon: 12–18 months. Re-rating + structural growth story, not a 90-day trade.

### High-Signal Disclosures

1. **TPU hardware sales — new business line.** Selling TPU hardware to select customers for own-datacenter deployment. Small 2026 revenue; vast majority in 2027. Already in the $462B backlog. Customers cited: Hudson River Trading, Thinking Machines Lab, Boston Dynamics.
2. **Cloud backlog mechanics.** $462B, >50% recognizes in next 24 months → ~$29B/quarter avg vs. $20B in Q1. Implies significant Cloud revenue acceleration ahead.
3. **AI Solutions revenue.** Cloud AI-model products grew ~800% Y/Y. 330 customers each processed >1T tokens in last 12 months; 35 hit 10T tokens. New customer acquisition doubled; $100M–$1B deals doubled Y/Y.
4. **Search durability.** +19% accelerating on retail + finance verticals. Schindler: "upside in coverage number" (currently ~20%) — AI monetizing longer/complex queries that were previously un-monetizable. 30%+ of customer search spend now uses AI-enabled campaigns.
5. **Universal Commerce Protocol (UCP).** Added Amazon, Meta, Microsoft, Salesforce, Stripe to tech council. Ulta Beauty launched commerce within AI Mode. Becoming industry standard.
6. **Gemini app monetization coming.** Schindler: format that works in AI Mode "would transfer successfully to Gemini app." Not yet timed.
7. **Other Bets restructuring.** Verily deconsolidated (external capital raise). GFiber to deconsolidate in Q4. Capital concentrating on Waymo.
