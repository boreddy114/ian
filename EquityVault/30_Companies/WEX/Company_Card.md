## One-Liner
WEX is a Fortress-class fleet card and HSA platform trading at 8-9x forward earnings because its board has refused to run the Corpay playbook — a compliance-embedded moat with physical closed-loop network infrastructure, verification-grade fraud data, and HSA custodial lock-in that the market prices as a commodity processor, with an identifiable governance catalyst on May 5, 2026.

## Bull Case
1. **Fortress moat confirmed.** Post-pressure-test: Classical 3.7, Agentic 2.2. Compliance-embedded SoR (Tier 4), physical closed-loop network (Tier 4-5), verification-grade fraud data (Tier 4). Market implies 0-year CAP; framework supports 10+ years.
2. **Margin recovery is a documented playbook.** Corpay SMB strategy evidence resolves the key question: SMB churn is identical (~17%) at both companies. Corpay manages it through outsourcing micro-accounts to resellers and aggressive mid-market pricing. WEX can replicate this exactly — it requires governance change, not product investment.
3. **Benefits separation unlocks 30-60% SOTP upside.** Benefits at implied ~7x inside WEX vs. HealthEquity at ~15x. Cross-sell synergies disproven by survey (only 6% overlap). Stranded costs manageable ($16-46M).
4. **Governance catalyst May 5, 2026.** Impactive nominees (Kurt Adams — ran Corpay's fleet business, Ellen Alemany — bank regulatory expertise, Lauren Taylor Wolfe — SOTP execution). Board vote is the near-term catalyst.
5. **FCF floor.** 12-14% FCF yield accrues regardless of board outcome. $2B+ investable cash over next 3 years.
6. **FMC distribution channel.** 30-40% of enterprise fleets >1,000 vehicles default to WEX co-branded programs via FMC contracts (10+ year tenures). Unscored moat that reinforces enterprise lock-in.

## Bear Case / Honest Weaknesses
1. **SMB structural erosion confirmed.** AtoB, Coast, Mudflap winning existing WEX customers in 10-100 vehicle segment. 200-vehicle fleet terminated WEX contract for AtoB. SMB churn 10-20% annually. Highest-margin tier eroding.
2. **Mobility margin gap to Corpay widening.** 24-point EBIT margin gap (WEX 24% vs. Corpay 48%). Gap widened every year 2021-2025. Q1'26 segment AOI margin compressed another 330bps.
3. **EV transition is execution risk, not tailwind.** Customer ultimatums: "I'm going to take all business away" if no unified fuel+EV billing. Cross Roads adoption data not disclosed. Home charging attribution bypasses closed-loop network.
4. **Visa Fleet 2.0.** Technically live for Level III data on open-loop rails. Merchant/acquirer adoption is bottleneck but has 2-3 year convergence timeline. If major fuel retailers adopt, keystone of network moat has defined shelf life.
5. **Corporate Payments sub-target after a decade.** $708M+ in M&A (eNett, AOC, Noventis) into wrong sub-segment (Embedded Payments). Organic growth -3% in 2025 vs. management's 10-15% target.
6. **FDIC consent order.** WEX Bank under consent order since Sept 2023 (30+ months). Only industrial bank with multiple enforcement actions.

## Catalyst Path
1. **May 5, 2026 annual meeting.** Board vote on Impactive nominees. 2-3 seats = credible mechanism for Benefits separation, Mobility pricing discipline. 0 seats = re-underwrite on Benefits-plus-fuel only.
2. **Benefits strategic review.** Any announcement of separation process / banker engagement.
3. **Mobility segment AOI margin trajectory.** Move toward 28-30% = pricing discipline taking hold.
4. **Fuel price realization.** WEX has ~4x fuel flow-through of CPAY. $3.70/gal FY assumption above NYMEX curve.
5. **FDIC consent order resolution.** Termination or modification would remove overhang.
