## 2026-04-10 — Catalini Moat Screen

**Source:** ServiceNow Catalini Moat Screen.docx
**Rating:** Neutral

### Assessment: CONTESTED, leaning Fortress
- Weighted Classical: 3.7/5.0 (Strong)

- SoR Bifurcation: ~55% Category A / ~45% Category B
- K_IP Bifurcation: ~35% Execution / ~65% Verification-Grade

### Core Insight
ServiceNow's moat is real but bifurcated. ~55% of switching cost is automatable migration labor, while the durable 45% (compliance, CMDB institutional memory, coordination) is genuinely defensible and being thrown out with the bathwater by the market. The stock's ~50% decline over-discounts the agentic threat.

### Five Moat Layers
1. **System of Record (Primary, Classical 4.3, Agentic 2.8):** CMDB as operational spine. 98% renewal. Compliance (SOX/HIPAA) and counterparty coordination anchor ~45% of switching cost in Category B.
2. **Ground Truth / Data (Secondary, Classical 3.6, Agentic 2.4):** CMDB is verification-grade K_IP — teaches systems what to reject, not just what to do. Compounds with time.
3. **Network Effects (Secondary, Classical 3.2, Agentic 2.6):** Complementary ecosystem (500+ Store apps, deep SI partnerships). Cross-departmental adoption = coordination equilibrium.
4. **Talent (Tertiary, 2.8/3.0):** Input, not moat itself.
5. **Brand (Tertiary, 3.0/2.2):** Emerging Schelling point for enterprise workflow automation.

### Shared Vulnerability: Wrapper/disintermediation. If Copilot/agents control UI, NOW becomes "system of storage." Counter: AI Control Tower positions NOW as governance layer for agents.
