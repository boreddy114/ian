## One-Liner
GE Vernova is the global power infrastructure franchise riding the AI-driven electricity super-cycle — $163B backlog, 100 GW of gas turbines under contract, and an Electrification segment inflecting to 18%+ EBITDA margins, offset by a still-money-losing Wind business that management is running for cash, not growth.

## Business Mix

|---|---|---|---|---|

## Bull Case
1. **Backlog explosion** — $163B total ($76B equipment +67% YoY), targeting $200B by 2027 (pulled forward a year); book-to-bill ~2.0x in Q1.
2. **Pricing power** — Gas turbine orders priced 10-20% higher than Q4 2025; 3-year lead times create scarcity premium.
3. **Electrification inflection** — Q1 data center orders ($2.4B) exceeded full-year 2025; $42B Electrification backlog up from $9B at YE'22; Prolec at >20% margins.
4. **Cash generation step-change** — Q1 FCF of $4.8B exceeded all of FY2025 ($3.7B); FY26 guide raised to $6.5-7.5B.
5. **Margin expansion** — Consolidated EBITDA margins +390 bps YoY in Q1; guidance raised +1pp at both ends (12-14%).
6. **AI demand flywheel** — ~20% of gas turbine customers are data centers; first integrated EMS order signals $200-300M+ entitlement per GW.
7. **Capacity moat** — Only ~10 GW remaining cumulative for '29-'30; reaching 20 GW annualized output by mid-2026, targeting 24 GW by 2028.

## Bear Case / Honest Weaknesses
1. **Wind losses persist** — ~$400M EBIT loss guided for 2026; offshore execution risk through 2027 (Dogger Bank).
2. **Tariff exposure** — $250-350M net impact in 2026 from Section 232 tariffs on components.
3. **Capacity constraints** — Sold-out through late decade limits upside flexibility and creates customer frustration.
4. **No framework agreements closed** — 30-35 in negotiation but zero finalized; execution risk on largest deals.
5. **Valuation stretch** — $297B market cap at ~$1,106/share; ~77x FY26E EPS of ~$14.38 prices in significant multi-year execution.
6. **Customer EPC/permitting bottlenecks** — Often the gating item, not turbine availability; creates revenue recognition timing risk.

## Key Monitoring

4. Wind segment path to breakeven (targeting ~2027).
5. FCF conversion sustainability post-Q1 timing benefit.
6. Nuclear SMR milestones (TVA Clinch River NRC license expected 2H 2026; OPG Darlington installation).
