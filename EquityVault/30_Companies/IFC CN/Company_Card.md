## Bull Case
1. **Cdn Personal Auto is the cleanest hard-market story** — 9% premium growth on 1% unit growth = 8pts pure pricing; underlying LR improving; structurally accretive to ROE for 12+ months.
2. **$4.0B excess capital margin** — $1.5B above "normal" operating level; NCIB renewed, buyback pace accelerating (~$650M annualized); management explicitly says stock is "meaningfully undervalued."
3. **Best-in-class management team** — Brindamour track record on capital allocation and M&A; consistent narrative not yet damaged.
4. **US segment outperforming** — 83.4% C/R, 4% growth in constant currency on sub-90% lines.
5. **Investment income strength** — Operating NII +10% y/y to $457M; 2026 NII guidance raised to ~$1.7B from $1.6B.

## Bear Case / Honest Weaknesses
1. **Underlying loss ratio going the wrong way** — +1.2pts y/y at consolidated level; +4.5pts in Cdn Commercial; +2.8pts in UK&I.
2. **PYD doing unsustainable heavy lifting** — 7.1% favorable vs. management's 2-4% normalized guidance. Strip PYD to 4% and group C/R is ~94.4%, not 91.3%.
3. **UK&I went backwards** — 103.2% C/R (+5.6pts y/y); management's "trend toward 90% within 12 months" credibility stretched after going backwards 10pts in one quarter.
4. **Cdn Commercial is the canary** — DPW growth slowed to +2% from 5%+ trajectory; underlying LR +4.5pts masked by 12.3% favorable PYD on long-tail lines.
5. **Expense ratio creeping** — +1.0pt y/y from growth investments and UK&I systems spend.
6. **Multiple refuses to expand** — strong reported numbers, no multiple expansion, share price stuck. Market has been right to keep the multiple at this level.
