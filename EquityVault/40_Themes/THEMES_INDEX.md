## ACTIVE THEMES

### 1. Scarcity → Abstraction → Abundance (Core Framework)
Every technology cycle follows the same arc. We are in the scarcity phase of
the AI cycle. Model intelligence is commoditizing; physical constraints are the
binding layer. Own scarcity, avoid abundance until winner identification is
possible. The cascade has moved through chips → memory → energy/grid and is
now entering thermal, water, and materials.
**Folder:** `Scarcity_Framework/`
**Key files:** Tech Cycles whitepaper, Cascading Bottleneck, Thermal Management,
Capacity Ledger CALLOUTS, NVIDIA Orchestration Problem, Scarcity What to Do

### 2. Physical Tether / Cascading Physical Constraints
Bits have collided with atoms. Software without a physical tether faces AI
substitution risk. Companies with irreplaceable physical assets are harder to
disrupt. Three-bucket structure for thermal/water: VRT (thermal hardware),
XYL (water movement), VLTO (water quality). FCX (copper) benefits across all
buckets. Hyperscaler straddle: MSFT, AMZN, GOOG bridge scarcity and abundance.
**Folder:** `AI_Physical_Tether/`
**Key files:** AI Power Cycle Utilities Memo, Scarcity Straddlers Physical
Tethers IC Memo

### 3. Software Compression / Abundance Risk
Pure-play application software faces existential disruption from abundant AI.
Payments industry is the governing precedent — entire sectors compress to
6-8x. Exceptions require system-of-record, merchant-of-record, or regulatory
moat. Agentic commerce creates structural beneficiaries (V/MA, BKNG, SHOP)
and structural losers (no SoR/MoR, no regulatory moat).
**Folder:** `Software/`
**Key files:** Agent Readiness Gap Memo, Agentic Commerce_Fragmenting Discovery,
Software Frameworks AI Era, Terminal Value of AI-Disrupted Industries, SaaS
Framework Data, Marathon SaaS Troubles, Frontier memos

### 4. Market Broadening / Mag 7 De-concentration
Six Kantrowitz conditions aligned for first time this cycle. Top 10 stocks
exceed 40% of S&P market cap. P/E expansion era likely over — alpha shifts
to earnings surprise, revisions, yield, ROE. Reduce SPY, buy RSP/IJH/IWS,
rotate into cyclical value. 10-year above 4.25% breaks this thesis.
**Folder:** `Macro/`
**Key file:** 2026 Kantro Outlook.pdf

### 5. White-Collar Employment Disruption / Fiscal Doom Loop
JOLTS subcategories most AI-exposed have collapsed. AI only needs to push
unemployment to 6-8% to trigger credit event. Employment taxes are 50%+ of
federal receipts. True Interest Expense near 100% of receipts. Structural
long gold as fiscal hedge. Watch credit card delinquency in the 700-750
FICO band.
**Folder:** `Macro/`
**Key files:** White Collar Jobs Recession Tree-rings 2_26.pdf, Q4 2025 Lux
Outlook.pdf

### 6. Oil as Macro Override
Oil-equity correlation approaching -100%. Broadening thesis, P/E expansion,
Fed path — all conditional on oil staying well-behaved. Iran/Strait of Hormuz
is the specific risk. Energy overweight during spikes. Exogenous shock
corrections are the shortest of all correction types. Don't bottom-fish
until WTI rolls over.
**Folder:** `Macro/`
**Key files:** 2026 Kantro Outlook.pdf, Q4 2025 Lux Outlook.pdf

### 7. China Supply Chain / Critical Minerals
China controls the parts bin. 20 years swapping Treasuries for critical mineral
supply chains. Decade-long tailwind for domestic rare earth producers. Own GRID,
PAVE as reshoring beneficiaries. AI capability race is not cleanly separated
from Chinese strategic access.
**Folder:** `Macro/`
**Key file:** Q4 2025 Lux Outlook.pdf

### 8. AI Capex Return Reckoning (2027-2028)
Market rewards capex growth today. By 2027-2028 it demands returns. $2T in
datacenter commitments in pipeline. 3-year GPU depreciation yields -1.7% IRR.
Hyperscaler RoCE dilution ~30%. Not short today given ad revenue cushion —
but the return test is coming. Watch capex-to-revenue ratios obsessively.
**Folder:** `Scarcity_Framework/` and `Macro/`
